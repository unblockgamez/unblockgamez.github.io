How to Choose the Best Gaming Laptop

  • Performance: Look for a powerful processor (Intel Core i7/i9 or AMD Ryzen 7/9) and a high-end GPU (RTX 4060 and above).
  • Display: A high refresh rate (144Hz+), QHD or 4K resolution, and good color accuracy enhance gaming visuals.
  • Portability: Lighter laptops (like the Asus ROG Zephyrus G14) are great for gaming on the go.
  • Budget: Choose a laptop that meets your needs without overspending—Dell G16 and MSI Katana 15 are great budget options.
